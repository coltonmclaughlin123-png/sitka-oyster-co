About us

We’re building a farm that can deliver premium oysters and dependable service, season after season.

Our mission

Produce premium live-shell oysters that reflect Sitka’s cold, clean waters—while operating with long-term stewardship in mind.

How we farm

We use off-bottom gear designed for strong growth and efficient handling, with an emphasis on careful sorting, cleaning, and packing.

Relevant experience

Our partnership brings decades of Alaska commercial fishing experience, vessel operations, and marine fabrication overlap that translates directly to safe, reliable farm work.

Food safety & handling

We take a food safety mindset seriously—clean handling, cold chain awareness, and clear lot traceability. As we scale, our process is designed to support consistent quality and buyer confidence.
